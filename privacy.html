Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2025

Introduction

Fovly ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our speed reading application at fovly.org.

Information We Collect

Fovly is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Text you paste: Any text you paste into the app is processed entirely in your browser. We do not send, store, or access your text on any server.
  • Usage analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data such as page views and session duration to improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Device information: Basic device and browser information may be collected for compatibility and performance purposes.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Fovly service
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Understand general usage patterns

Data Storage

Fovly processes all reading content locally in your browser. We do not store your text on our servers. Reading preferences (such as speed settings) may be saved in your browser's local storage for convenience.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that collect anonymous usage data. These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

Cookies

Fovly may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Children's Privacy

Fovly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at fovly.org.
