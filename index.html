How It Works

Four steps to
faster reading

No setup needed. Just open the app and go.

1

Add a book

Import an EPUB or FB2 file, or pick a free classic from the built-in catalog. Fovly also accepts books shared from other apps.

2

Set your pace

Choose your words-per-minute with a slider or pick a preset. Start at 200 WPM and work your way up.

3

Read with AI assist

Words appear one at a time at your speed. Tap any word for instant AI explanations. Need a recap? AI retells what you've read.

4

Finish smarter

When you finish, take an AI comprehension quiz, then generate a full summary to lock in what you've learned.
