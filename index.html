How It Works

Three steps to faster reading

No setup needed. Just open the app and go.

1

Add a book

Import an EPUB, FB2, or TXT file from your device, or pick a free classic from the built-in catalog. Fovly also accepts books shared from other apps.

2

Set your pace

Choose your words-per-minute with a slider or pick a preset. Start at 200 WPM and work your way up as you build confidence.

3

Press play and read

Words appear one at a time at a fixed focal point. Your eyes stay still while your brain absorbs the text. Tap to pause, swipe to navigate.
